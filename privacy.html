Last updated: April 2026

Terre de Chance ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit terrechance.com or contact us.

Your information is stored securely and accessed only by authorised personnel. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at knock-knock@terrechance.com.
